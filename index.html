E - Eternal Tides (Lv. 10)

Awaken Moonbound Tides, dealing AoE Hydro DMG and summoning Gravity Ripple.
Gravity Ripple will follow the active character on the field, while continuously dealing AoE Hydro DMG to surrounding opponents. While Gravity Ripple is on the field, and nearby party members trigger Lunar reactions or inflict Lunar Reaction DMG, Columbina will accrue a special Gravity. Once Gravity is accumulated to its limit, different types of damage will be dealt to enemies based on the type of Lunar Reaction that has accumulated the most Gravity for Columbina.

The Eternal Moon's orbit guides the rise and fall of the tides. With a languid motion, the goddess lets her hand fall, drawing forth a ripple across the Primordial Sea, the great ocean, and numberless crowds.

Passives

Call of the Moon

When triggering Kuuvahki Interference, Columbina gains the Lunacy effect, increasing her CRIT Rate by 5% for 10s. This effect can be stacked up to 3 times.

Law of the New Moon

When characters within the Lunar Domain trigger Lunar reactions, they will gain the following effects respectively:
- Lunar-Charged: Thundercloud attack interval is reduced by 0.5s.
- Lunar-Bloom: When triggering Lunar-Bloom reactions, the party gains a special Moonridge Dew. Up to 3 Moonridge Dews can be gained every 18s.
- Lunar-Crystallize: When Moondrift Harmony is triggered, every Moondrift has a 33% chance to inflict an extra attack.

Moonsign Benediction: Borrowed Moonlight

When a party member triggers an Electro-Charged/Bloom/Hydro-Crystallize reaction, it will be converted into a Lunar-Charged/Lunar-Bloom/Lunar-Crystallize reaction. Lunar Reaction DMG inflicted by party members gain a Base DMG increase based on Columbina's Max HP. For every 1,000 points of Max HP, the Base DMG of Lunar reactions is increased by 0.2%, up to a maximum of 7%.

Additionally, when Columbina is in the party, the party's Moonsign will increase by 1 level.

Lunar Vigil

While in the open world of Nod-Krai, and an active character dies, The Damselette will use trilunar authority to revive the party member and restore their HP. The amount of HP recovered depends on The Damselette's Friendship level. This effect can trigger up to once every 100s.

Constellations

1 - Radiance Over Blossoms and Peaks

Unleashing her Elemental Skill Eternal Tides will immediately trigger an effect equivalent to the effect of Kuuvahki Interference. If no Gravity has been gained, this Kuuvahki Interference will be considered to have gained maximum Gravity from Lunar-Charged reactions.

Moonsign: Ascendant Gleam

When Kuuvahki Interference is triggered, if the Lunar reaction type that has accumulated the most Gravity for Columbina is:
- Lunar-Charged: Current active character will recover 6 Energy.
- Lunar-Bloom: Active character's interruption resistance will be increased for 8s.
- Lunar-Crystallize: Summons a Rainsea Shield. The Rainsea Shield's DMG absorption is based on 12% of Columbina's Max HP, and it will absorb Hydro DMG with 250% effectiveness for 8s.

All nearby party members' Lunar Reaction DMG is elevated by 3%.

2 - Not in Lone Splendor

Rate of accumulating Gravity increases by 34%. When Kuuvahki Interference is triggered, Columbina will receive the Lunar Brilliance effect, which increases her Max HP by 30% for 8s.

Moonsign: Ascendant Gleam

When Lunar Brilliance is active, if, during the current trigger of Kuuvahki Interference, the Lunar reaction type that has accumulated the most Gravity for Columbina is:
- Lunar-Charged: The party's current active character gains ATK equal to 0.7% of Columbina's Max HP.
- Lunar-Bloom: Elemental Mastery of the party's current active character increases. Increase in Elemental Mastery is equal to 0.35% of Columbina's Max HP.
- Lunar-Crystallize: DEF of the party's current active character increases. Increase in DEF is equal to 0.7% of Columbina's Max HP.

All nearby party members' Lunar Reaction DMG is elevated by 3%.

3 - Dreamlike Glow Across Tranquil Waters

Increases the Level of Elemental Skill Eternal Tides by 3.
Maximum upgrade level is 15.

All nearby party members' Lunar Reaction DMG is elevated by 3%.

4 - Cloudveiled Ridges in Floral Mists

When Kuuvahki Interference is triggered, 4 Elemental Energy will be restored to Columbina;
Additionally, if the Lunar reaction type that accumulates the most Gravity for Columbina is Lunar-Charged, Lunar-Bloom, or Lunar-Crystallize, the current trigger of Kuuvahki Interference will deal greater Lunar Reaction DMG. Increase in DMG is equal to 12.5%/2.5%/12.5% of Columbina's Max HP respectively. This effect can be triggered at most once every 15s.

All nearby party members' Lunar Reaction DMG is elevated by 3%.

5 - Silence Tending One Lone Song

Increases the Level of Elemental Burst Moonlit Melancholy by 3.
Maximum upgrade level is 15.

All nearby party members' Lunar Reaction DMG is elevated by 3%.

6 - Through Darkness Led by Moonlight

For 8s after characters in the Lunar Domain trigger a Lunar reaction, based on the element involved in the reaction, the CRIT DMG of the corresponding Elemental DMG is increased by 80%. The CRIT DMG-increasing effects for the same Elemental Type do not stack.

All nearby party members' Lunar Reaction DMG is elevated by 5%.
